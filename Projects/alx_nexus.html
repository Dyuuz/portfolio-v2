Project Overview

StockBridge is a multi-vendor e-commerce platform in development, giving vendors a straightforward way to list and manage products, and giving customers a fast, reliable shopping experience from browsing to checkout. It supports product catalogs across multiple categories, vendor-managed inventory with real-time stock tracking, and a full cart-to-checkout purchase flow.

The platform is built around reliability, fair vendor access, and a smooth buying experience. Vendors can onboard, list products with pricing and discounts, and monitor stock. Customers can browse categories, manage a cart, complete checkout, and track order status and history. Payment processing is currently in active development and not yet complete.

Use cases include: multi-vendor product marketplace, vendor inventory management, customer checkout flow, and role-based marketplace access (customer/vendor/admin).

Current status: Customer and vendor onboarding, role-based access control, product catalog, cart, and checkout are complete. Only payment integration is in progress.

StockBridge is best positioned as a practical foundation for vendors who want to sell online without friction, and customers who want a dependable path from browsing to a confirmed order.
